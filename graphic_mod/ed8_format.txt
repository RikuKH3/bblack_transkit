0x0	(10 bytes)	magic ('.8Bit'+$8D5D8CCB00)
0xA	(2 bytes)	ed8 type (always $0001)
0xC	(2 bytes)	unknown1 (always 0)
0xE	(2 bytes)	width
0x10	(2 bytes)	height
0x12	(2 bytes)	number of colors in palette
0x14	(2 bytes)	unknown2 (always 0) (positioning related?)
0x16	(4 bytes)	image size in bytes
0x1A	(x bytes)	palette (3 bytes per color)
0x1A+x	(y bytes)	image data

Use 'Crass' or 'Susie' with 'Ifactive.spi' plugin to convert ED8/SAL and EDT images into BMP.
After modifying BMP convert it into uncompressed ED8.
You can replace EDT files with ED8. For example: delete E000.EDT and place E000.ED8 instead.
For smart color reduction and best image quality use OPTPiX iMageStudio.
